This vintage radio was designed by Gregory Lhomme-Hounsfield, and was based off of a real French radio from the 70s. The leftmost and smallest dial toggles the music tone preset, to the right of that is the marche(ON) button. The The small dials to the right of the power button on top correspond to different radio wavebands, ultra-shortwave (FM/VHF), shortwave, longwave, and medium wave (AM). These dials can be used to switch between bands. The larger knobs on the right correspond to recherche (search/tuning), and volume.
