This cassette player came with a power adapter, but could be played off a set of four AA batteries. When the cassette tape was put inside the player, you could record and play back audio, as well as fast forward, rewind, and stop playback and recording.

This device has no display, so you had to guess where you thought you were on the tape while rewinding or fast forwarding.

They held 30 to 45 minutes of audio per side, and being double sided doubled the amount of music it could hold.
