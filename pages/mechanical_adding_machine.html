The adding machine was a revolutionary product in terms of business, as beforehand people had to add manually many different totals in large transactions.

It may have been limited to addition, but that didn't stop businesses the country over from using the early calculators in most, if not all of their transactions.

The machine pictured below is of a Model 3, made from 1910-1911. The ink ribbon still works after all this time, so feel free to give it a whirl while it's wet!
