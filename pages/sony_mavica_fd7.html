This camera was one of the first digital cameras to support proper optical zoom (10x). It was also the second digital camera Sony made. It could take pictures at 640 x 480 resolution, the upper limit of standard definition.

The FD in its name stands for Floppy Disk, as 3 & 1/2 inch floppies (pictured up above) could be used with the camera.
