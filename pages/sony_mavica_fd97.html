This camera could take HD photos (1024 x 70) and write them to either a memory stick, or a floppy drive.

You could record 5 second long videos with a floppy drive inserted, or longer with a memory stick.

It had image stabilization capabilities, allowing users with shaky hands to take photos without worrying about motion blur.
