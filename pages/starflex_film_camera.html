A film camera by Kodak. It was priced new at just $10, and used rolls of film to store pictures, which needed to be taken to a store to be developed.

It came with a one-use flash bulb, and one roll of film. It could also switch between taking color, and black & white photos.

It was marketed as a simple, easy-to-use camera for the amateur photographer. For someone who doesn't want to carry about the expensive, weighty, but highly advanced cameras for the time.
